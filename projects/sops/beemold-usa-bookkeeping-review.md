## Monthly close process

1. **Add the Mercury + BoA transactions manually.** The **bank-feed sync has been broken since
   Feb 2025** → transactions are **uploaded manually** (can be done weekly; Maria does it at
   month-end). Reference: [Add Mercury & BoA transactions manually](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/13RxfIFN7hWxwImviqoqlmSvuI_W_uCSC)
2. **Bank reconciliation.** Reconcile every feed (Mercury checking + savings + CC, BoA). Reference: [Bookkeeping walkthrough](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1Jhnl69pdY6eRzEYSGuUtHIDVw0WPTs9h) · [Bank statements](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1svndC4S3nqUEqDsv5eqq-KXU1VF-7_LG)
3. **Intercompany loan review.** Review the **loan balance between this entity and its sister
   company** (same owner) and keep the two sides in agreement.
4. **Close gate.** The triage / **Uncategorized** accounts must read **$0** before the month
   is called closed. Deliver bundled with the sister entity (Balance Sheet always; P&L when
   there's activity). Reference: [Maria's Beemold doc guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1n2SuSEym5am-TwORAG66YxCQSXLwsd1YYzLJLm_3Sco/edit)

## Categorization rules

These override any QuickBooks auto-suggestion.

1. **Bank-feed sync broken since Feb 2025 → manual upload** of the Mercury + BoA transactions.
   Resolving the connection so manual upload can stop is an open item.
2. **Intercompany loan** — review the balance with the sister company each month and keep the
   two sides in agreement.
3. **Low activity** — the quieter of the pair; some months have no P&L activity (Balance Sheet
   still delivered). Keep the entities' books distinct.

## Monthly review checklist (what the reviewer verifies)

1. Mercury + BoA transactions uploaded manually (sync still broken).
2. Every feed reconciled (Mercury checking + savings + CC, BoA).
3. Intercompany loan balance with the sister company reviewed and agreed.
4. **Close gate:** triage / Uncategorized accounts read **$0**.
5. Reports issued together with the sister entity; annual report filed when due.
