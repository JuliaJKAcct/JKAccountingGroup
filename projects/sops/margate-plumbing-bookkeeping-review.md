## Monthly close process

1. **Bank feed / categorization.** Work the bank feed across every account. Reference: [Bank feed walkthrough](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1bFL0_FKhAT0oHOD-3P4-NL86eG_S7UeC/view)
2. **Bank reconciliation.** Reconcile every feed (WF 8477, Mercury checking + savings + CC,
   BoA 9866, Amex). Reference: [Reconciliation — part 1](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1DS0YwqYtTpmFXNKXXIDVkevXAlRueCm8/view) · [part 2](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1BdERtlCSW45EzUEHARfRKiOptTP-GgjT/view) · [Bank statements](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1eyczv1kx_tMKNocFt-kogFt4maFI4Nd3)
3. **Intercompany loan review.** Review the **loan balance between this entity and its sister
   company** (same owner) and keep the two sides in agreement.
4. **AR corrections.** AR is managed by the owner and is often inaccurate — expect to spend
   real time here and **bring open questions to the standing weekly meeting with Julia** (see
   *Categorization rules*).
5. **Adjust payments made outside Gusto.** Some employee payments are sent outside Gusto and
   must be adjusted in the books.
6. **Close gate.** The triage / **Uncategorized** accounts must read **$0** before the month
   is called closed. Reference: [Maria's Margate doc guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1zs5KoJAHtRpe5lTIEXTLJipt-FOaJLPm7fKynUpeE9I/edit)

## Categorization rules

These override any QuickBooks auto-suggestion.

1. **AR is managed by the owner and is not accurate.** He often books the wrong payment date,
   applies payments to the wrong invoices, or uses the wrong account — so **payment
   reconciliation is a recurring problem**. This is the reason for the **standing weekly
   meeting with Julia**; don't force a match you can't support.
2. **Employee payments outside Gusto** occur and must be adjusted in the books.
3. **Intercompany loan** — review the balance with the sister company each month and keep the
   two sides in agreement.
4. **1099 preparation** for subcontractors — track payees and W-9s in Double.

## Monthly review checklist (what the reviewer verifies)

1. Every feed reconciled (WF, Mercury checking + savings + CC, BoA, Amex).
2. Intercompany loan balance with the sister company reviewed and agreed.
3. AR corrections worked; open questions taken to the weekly Julia meeting.
4. Any outside-Gusto employee payments adjusted.
5. **Close gate:** triage / Uncategorized accounts read **$0**.
6. Reports issued together with the sister entity.
