## Monthly close process

Most activity is **transfers between accounts** plus card expenses (ads, software,
subscriptions, travel & meals), **sales**, and **labor**.

1. **Bank reconciliation.** Reconcile every feed (Chase checking + savings + 3 credit cards).
   The **sub-credit-card 4800 can't connect to QBO** → **add its transactions manually**. Reference: [Bookkeeping — part 1](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1vrdzDxXuUICisxke-bGpI5qyjwrsIDd-/view) · [Bank statements](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1lWvjU8GN0mVwrIRSc-V93o3eYrnjRGkW)
2. **Monthly Brokerage JE.** At the end of each month, book a **journal entry for the managed
   brokerage accounts from their statements**. JK has **no direct access** to those accounts —
   **request the statements via TaxDome**. Reference: [Bookkeeping — part 2 (Brokerage JE)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1R5KTxOsM5Jr-1jqzBdP9XXms1AuKECpa/view)
3. **Uncategorized workflow.** The owner clears "Uncategorized" quickly, so when you're unsure
   of a transaction, **send it to Uncat** for the owner to resolve rather than guessing.
4. **Close gate.** The triage / **Uncategorized** accounts must read **$0** before the month
   is called closed (after the owner has cleared what you sent to Uncat). Reference: [Maria's Sensustech doc guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aSKtLpHRerNcdo40S4deymcYrO7QNBujez2LMGKbPkQ/edit)

## Categorization rules

These override any QuickBooks auto-suggestion.

1. **Sub-CC 4800 is entered manually** — it cannot connect to QBO.
2. **Monthly Brokerage JE from statements** — the managed brokerage accounts have no live
   feed; JK books them from the statements (requested via TaxDome).
3. **Use Uncategorized rather than guessing** — the owner resolves Uncat items quickly.
4. **Typical activity:** transfers between accounts; ads / software / subscriptions / travel &
   meals on the credit cards; sales; labor.
5. **1099 preparation** is part of the engagement — track payees and W-9s in Double.

## Monthly review checklist (what the reviewer verifies)

1. Every feed reconciled (Chase checking + savings + 3 CCs); **sub-CC 4800 entered manually**.
2. **Brokerage JE** booked from the managed-brokerage statements.
3. Unsure items sent to Uncat and then resolved by the owner.
4. **Close gate:** triage / Uncategorized accounts read **$0**.
5. Monthly reports delivered (bundled with the sibling entity as applicable).
