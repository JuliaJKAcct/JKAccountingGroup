How to create your password & sign in

Your Client Portal doesn't have a "sign up" button — you create your password yourself, straight from the sign-in page, using "Forgot your password." It takes about two minutes, and you only do it once.

Your email is already on file with us, so it's usually filled in for you. Just follow these three screens 👇

1

On the sign-in page, choose to sign in with a password

If the page opens on "magic link," click Sign in with password near the bottom.

Open the email and set your password

In a few minutes you'll get an email from Double — check spam/promotions too. Open it, follow the link, and choose a password you'll remember.

That's it — this is how you'll sign in from now on

Next time, go to the same page, choose Sign in with password, and enter your email and the password you just created.

Didn't get the reset email? Wait a few minutes and check your spam and promotions folders. If it still hasn't arrived, just reply to us — we can send you a fresh link directly.
