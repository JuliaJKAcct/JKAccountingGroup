CLIENT PORTAL

Signing in to your Client Portal for the first time

Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

Your Client Portal doesn't have a "create account" step — the fastest, most reliable way in is to set your own password using the "Forgot your password" link, right from the sign-in page. It takes about two minutes and you'll only need to do it once.

01 Open your Client Portal sign-in page.
02 Click "Sign in with password."
03 On the password screen, click "Forgot your password?" (top right, next to the password field).
04 Confirm your email is in the box (it's usually filled in for you — if not, use the email you signed up with) and click "Get reset link."
05 Check your inbox — and spam/promotions, just in case — for an email from Double. Open it and follow the link to set your password. It can take a few minutes to arrive.
06 Once your password is set, that's it. Every time after this, go back to the sign-in page, choose "Sign in with password," and log in with your email and this password.
A couple of notes: we don't recommend "Sign in with magic link" — the email often doesn't arrive, and even when it does, the link expires and you'd need a new one every time. "Sign in with Google" isn't working for our portal right now either — please skip that button too. If the reset email never shows up, just reply here and we'll send you a new link directly.
