# SOP: Business Tax Receipt — City of Hollywood + Broward County

> **Status:** Active · **Owner:** Julia · **Last updated:** 2026-07-06

Getting a Florida Business Tax Receipt (BTR) for a business physically located
in Hollywood, FL (Broward County). This is the **quick runbook** — steps and
links only. For the research behind each step, verify-before-filing caveats,
and the full county application screen-by-screen, see the detailed guide:
[`client-services/business-tax-receipts/hollywood-broward-btr-guide.md`](../client-services/business-tax-receipts/hollywood-broward-btr-guide.md).

## Before you start — the one question that changes everything

**Will the owner reside at the business address?**
- **Yes** → it's a genuine home occupation. Proceed with the steps below; the
  Certificate of Use (CU) exemption for single-family/condo applies legitimately.
- **No / it's 100% commercial use of a residential address** → **stop and call
  City of Hollywood Planning & Urban Design first** (954-921-3471, opt. 2):
  *"I have a property zoned single-family residential at [address], where no one
  will reside — it will be used solely for a business. Am I permitted to operate
  this business there as-is, or do I need some kind of permit or approval first?"*
  We've confirmed by phone that Hollywood's residential zones are strict and
  don't offer a variance/special-exception path for this — don't assume it'll
  work out; get it in writing/by phone before doing anything else.

## Steps

1. **Foundations** — entity on Sunbiz (or DBA), EIN, and any required state
   license already in hand.
   Sunbiz: <https://sunbiz.org> · corp/LLC search: <https://search.sunbiz.org/Inquiry/CorporationSearch/ByName> · fictitious name search: <https://dos.sunbiz.org/ficinam.html>

2. **Broward County — apply via BTExpress** (do this before the city step;
   Hollywood requires proof of an active county account):
   <https://broward.county-taxes.com/btexpress>
   - Required Documentation (preview screen)
   - Fictitious (DBA) Name — registered DBA, or "doing business under full legal name"
   - Occupation Information — business description, lodging/restaurant/vending Y-N, employee count
   - Required Documentation questionnaire — 13 yes/no questions (unincorporated area, entity type, City of West Park, DBPR-regulated, contractor competency, Dept. of Agriculture, Dept. of Financial Services, **Office of Financial Regulation**, Healthcare Administration, auto body/moving, attorney, County Health Dept., other state agency) — a "Yes" determines which state/county license to upload
   - Submit → confirm via the emailed link → **24–48h** processing
   - Regulator links if triggered: DBPR <https://www.myfloridalicense.com> · Office of Financial Regulation <https://flofr.gov>

3. **City of Hollywood — apply for the City BTR**:
   <https://apps.hollywoodfl.org/LbtrOnline/>
   - Needs: active Broward County account (step 2), Sunbiz entity/DBA docs, proof
     of the location, state license if regulated, Certificate of Use (unless
     single-family/condo and genuinely home-based — see gate above), **$25
     non-refundable processing fee + local business tax** (amount set at
     submission).
   - Info page: <https://hollywoodfl.org/218/Local-Business-Tax>

4. **Post the receipt(s)** at the place of business and **calendar the Sept 30
   renewal** (business tax year runs Oct 1–Sep 30; first-time receipts issued on
   or after Apr 1 pay only half the annual tax).
