## Monthly close process

1. **Bookkeeping review & reconciliation.** The GM categorizes almost everything; **JK
   reconciles** each feed and supports the GM on unclear items (email + screenshots). Send the
   monthly **AP and AR reports** for the GM to review and flag any write-offs, plus any
   unclear bill payment or deposit found during reconciliation. Reference: [Bookkeeping — part 1](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1OjTAC2XLxxPpm9f47t-ib_e8PuwPP2qu/view) · [part 2](https://drive.google.com/file/d/18ZNwVeh_RzbGuhvAMKqQM2LSvxl2-VKN/view) · [part 3](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1T-VRY_FDr-zk4FjOBDeibjpK-osef-Md/view) · [Bank statements](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1sfeBSX2o_nh1ROKoGySRAJitTApsbIa1)
2. **Sales tax — monthly (FL DOR).** JK files and pays. **The portal does not produce the
   correct report — recalculate it every month.** Common causes to fix in the invoices ("see
   math"): overridden sales-tax amount, product marked non-taxable, wrong item, or
   address/location error. **Any doubt → escalate to Julia** (often a Zoom review together). Reference: [Sales Taxes walkthrough](https://drive.google.com/file/d/101Rfaf3Ej8abMsiyIQQ2RlgGpctwnIOl/view) · [Sales Taxes folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1pqw3HhrpvRtPRdyZU5I4vM4WqBDxCl7q)
3. **Payroll — Gusto, biweekly (Tuesday).** The GM emails notes through the pay period
   (commissions, weekend shifts, extra hours). **Tuesday morning: send one consolidated email
   to the GM (CC Julia)** summarizing all of it and asking if anything else is pending.
   Standing rules: weekend shifts pay a fixed per-employee rate; PTO logged as "day without
   payment" is rejected in Gusto and the hours simply removed from the period; one contractor
   is **skipped** until the GM says to resume; one employee is hourly (approve + sync time);
   anything extra → **Other earnings → Commissions**, extra hours → **Regular Hours**. **After
   each payroll, submit the contribution roster to the American Funds Simple IRA.** Reference: [Payroll walkthrough](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1PJHAzfPw41lV_SFA6qN_WtJJbyE_1wvW/view)
4. **Close gate.** The triage / **Uncategorized** accounts must read **$0** before the month
   is called closed — buyback transactions are the usual culprit (see below). Reference: [Maria's Mobilesource doc guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1XfMfe3pMxG9OjoawnrPuK2Hf3Hlqvz3i-Q98weTI9j0/edit)

## Categorization rules

These override any QuickBooks auto-suggestion.

1. **Split responsibility.** The GM categorizes ~99% in QBO; JK reconciles and reviews.
   Communicate by **email + screenshots**, not live shared work.
2. **Buyback transactions repeatedly land in "Uncategorized expenses"** and must be re-mapped
   (a buyback clearing account is zeroed with a matching Bill + Invoice) — a recurring pain
   point and a strong candidate to standardize.
3. **The FL DOR sales-tax report is never right out of the box** — always recalculate; watch
   the invoice-level "see math" errors (rule in step 2 above).
4. **1099 preparation** is part of the engagement — track payees and W-9s in Double.

## Monthly review checklist (what the reviewer verifies)

1. Every feed reconciled (Chase ×2, Valley Bank ×2, Amex, Costco/Citi Visa).
2. AP/AR reports sent to the GM; write-offs and unclear items resolved.
3. Sales tax recalculated, filed, and paid via the FL DOR portal.
4. Payroll run (consolidated Tuesday email sent, CC Julia); Simple IRA roster submitted.
5. Buyback transactions re-mapped out of Uncategorized.
6. **Close gate:** triage / Uncategorized accounts read **$0**.
7. Monthly reports issued.
