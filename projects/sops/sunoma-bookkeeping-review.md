## Monthly close process

The close can't finish until the owner sends the month-end reports. Each step links to
Maria's walkthrough (or the working file) in Drive.

1. **Gather the month-end reports (TaxDome).** The owner sends, via **TaxDome**, a **Monthly
   Ledger Report, an Expense report, and a Cash-on-Hand** figure for **both stores (LP / AP)**.
   Save them to Drive. Reference: [Save reports & Cash-on-Hand](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1-oxhbj1STk8LHmUQLj8nXzxTjSi4eqb4)
2. **Bank reconciliation.** Reconcile every feed (Truist ×2, Chase CC, Truist Visa, PayPal). Reference: [Bank reconciliation](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1uf1eW9kE6SkelJ_YmWmuDjykIiezS7SJ) · [Bank-feed categorizing](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1iDAbuodacGVBlbEA45XU1WNIprhcZURB)
3. **Change Merch (PaymentsHub) deposit / fee dates.** Correct the merchant deposit and fee
   dates so they land in the right period. Reference: [Change Merch dates](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1UUCL7h9ldxBo4tdmLklS1_-qrvxKQTL_) · [PaymentsHub folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1OslVl09HYcinJSzE3LVaClu8MdJAjzNw)
4. **ADP — rename the JE names, then reconcile.** Rename the ADP journal entries to the house
   naming (`ADP MM-DD-YY` + `MM-DD-YY PayrollClear`), then reconcile ADP into the books. Reference: [Change ADP JE names](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/14w3F6EKlLAEX_nEVhHWJnVtsD59V0ao7) · [ADP reconciliation](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1WfaWYIhs24Ej3J4kJaFAoeV9xBbxPy6p)
5. **Monthly JEs, per store.** Book: Monthly JE (LP), Monthly JE (AP), Expenses JE (LP),
   Expenses JE (AP), Cash-on-Hand adjustment, Inventory adjustment, Deposit-Fee (MRCH) AP,
   Deposit-Fee (MRCH) LP. Reference: [Monthly JEs](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1S2m67LSh4RYPI7eDCFez7uzpnrap4pd9) · [JE workbook](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/17HlEtoC8hLK-neyvtqX-f5KYx_iFAE9U/edit) · [Reports folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1AvtAxO6QGGOSYCOR6V2Eq2fqtRglPGXn)
6. **Close gate.** The triage / **Uncategorized** accounts must read **$0** before the month
   is called closed. Then deliver only on the quarter or on request. Reference: [Sunoma Process (master sheet)](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13jpUI-2MY9dEsFsYzia8s7028Pehvl3uyzrNNFI6VCc/edit)

## Categorization rules

These override any QuickBooks auto-suggestion.

1. **Everything is per store.** LP (Lucky Pawn) and AP (Auto Pawn) are reconciled and
   journalized **separately** every month; keep the two stores' entries distinct.
2. **PaymentsHub (Merch) deposit / fee dates** routinely need correcting during the close so
   deposits and fees fall in the right period.
3. **"Teller Cashed" items — check the attachments** before categorizing.
4. **ADP JEs get renamed** to the house naming before reconciling.
5. **1099 preparation** is part of the engagement — track payees and W-9s in Double.

## Monthly review checklist (what the reviewer verifies)

1. Month-end reports received from the owner (Ledger, Expense, Cash-on-Hand) for **both** stores.
2. Every feed reconciled (Truist ×2, Chase CC, Truist Visa, PayPal).
3. Merch (PaymentsHub) deposit/fee dates corrected.
4. ADP JEs renamed and reconciled.
5. All per-store JEs booked (Monthly LP/AP, Expenses LP/AP, Cash-on-Hand, Inventory, Deposit-Fee AP/LP).
6. **Close gate:** triage / Uncategorized accounts read **$0**.
7. Reports sent only on the quarter or on request (no monthly send).
