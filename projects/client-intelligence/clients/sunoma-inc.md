### Bookkeeping & monthly close
- **Applies?** Yes — **monthly close**; **reports go to the client quarterly / on request** (no monthly report needed). To speed it up, move the pipeline status in the first 3 days of the month so the owner sends the month-end reports faster. _(Maria's doc guide, master plan)_
- **Process notes (→ future SOP):** the month-end sequence Maria documented — reports are split **per store (LP / AP)**. At month-end the owner sends, via **TaxDome**, a **Monthly Ledger Report, an Expense report, and a Cash-on-Hand** figure for **both** stores.
  1. **Bank reconciliation** of every feed (Truist ×2, Chase CC, Truist Visa, PayPal).
  2. **Change Merch (PaymentsHub) deposit / fee dates** so they land in the right period.
  3. **Change / rename the ADP JE names**, then **reconcile ADP**.
  4. **Monthly JEs, per store:** Monthly JE (LP), Monthly JE (AP), Expenses JE (LP), Expenses JE (AP), Cash-on-Hand adjustment, Inventory adjustment, Deposit-Fee (MRCH) AP, Deposit-Fee (MRCH) LP.
