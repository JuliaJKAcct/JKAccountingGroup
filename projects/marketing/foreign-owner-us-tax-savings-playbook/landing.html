Straight answers to the fair questions

I already have an accountant. Why do I need you?

Most accountants are preparers — they file what already happened. A lot of foreign owners have never had someone plan with them during the year, which is where the savings actually live. The guide is free, and a second look costs you nothing. If your current setup is already optimal, we'll tell you — that's a good outcome too.

Is this "aggressive"? Will it get me in trouble?

No. Everything here is ordinary, legitimate planning — the rules used the way they're meant to be used. We keep you compliant while we save (the foreign-owner form, sales tax, worker rules still matter). We don't do schemes. If a strategy only works when no one looks closely, it isn't one we'd suggest.

Honestly, we can't promise a number from a guide — your savings live in your real books, not a quiz. That's exactly what the free review is for: we look at your actual numbers and give you the specific figure and the levers behind it. If there's nothing to save, we'll say so.
