Answer these seven questions

Just say yes or no. Nothing is saved and no email is needed.

1. Do you have a separate bank account just for your business?

Not mixed together with your personal money.

2. Are your business records up to date within the last month?

3. Do you keep your receipts and bills, on paper or on your computer?

4. Is every business cost sorted into a type (like rent, supplies, travel)?

5. Do your records match your bank statements each month?

This is called reconciling — checking your books against the bank.

6. Did you file last year's taxes on time?

7. Do you know your profit for this year so far?
