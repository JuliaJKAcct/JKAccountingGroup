Why this matters

In plain words, so you know what is at stake.

  • An S‑Corp owner must take a salary. The rest of your money can be business profit. But the salary has to come first, and it has to be fair.
  • "Fair" means the market value of your work. It is roughly what you would pay someone else to do your job. Not too high, not too low.
  • Pay too little and the IRS can charge back taxes and a penalty. This is one of the first things they check on an S‑Corp. A figure you can show your work on keeps you safe.
