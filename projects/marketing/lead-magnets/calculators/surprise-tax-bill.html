What new owners miss

In plain words, so nothing catches you off guard.

  • No one takes tax out for you. At a job, your employer sends tax to the IRS from each paycheck. When you work for yourself, that is now your job.
  • The IRS wants it four times a year. Not once. If you wait until tax time, the bill can be large — and there can be a penalty for paying late.
  • You can plan for it. When you set money aside as you earn, tax time is calm — no surprise, no scramble. That is what we set up for you.
