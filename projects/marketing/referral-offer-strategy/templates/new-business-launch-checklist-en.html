Starting Your Business Right — A Financial Readiness Checklist

You just committed to a space — congratulations. Before day one, walk through this list. Nothing here needs to be perfect yet; it just needs to be on your radar.

01Entity & structure

Is your business formed as the right entity for how you'll operate (LLC, S-Corp, etc.)? If you elected S-Corp status, was it filed on time?

02EIN & local registrations

Federal EIN in hand, plus any state and local filings for this location — including a city/county Business Tax Receipt.

03Business bank account

A dedicated business account, kept separate from personal funds from day one.

04Bookkeeping

A system in place before the first transaction hits — and someone who'll actually keep it current, not reconstruct it later.

05Sales tax (if applicable)

Registered to collect and remit before the first taxable sale, not after.

06Payroll (if hiring at launch)

Set up correctly before the first pay run — worker classification, filings, the basics.

07Estimated taxes

Know that quarterly payments may apply to you now, and when they're due — before the first one is missed.

08Set up to grow

Books structured to show what actually matters for this business — not one lump "Sales" account you'll have to untangle later.
