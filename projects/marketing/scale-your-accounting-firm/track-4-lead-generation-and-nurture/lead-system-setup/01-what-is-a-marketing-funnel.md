# Video 1 — What Is a Marketing Funnel?

**Track:** 4 — Lead Generation & Nurture · **Module:** Lead System Setup
**Type:** Informative / overview (no actionable items — sets up vocabulary and
the system for the rest of the module)

## Core definition

A marketing funnel takes a **broad, unqualified group of strangers** and
narrows them down, step by step, into a **small group of qualified buyers**.
Volume in, qualification at each stage, revenue out. The goal isn't to work
with everybody — it's to narrow the pool in a way that's profitable at the end.

## The full pipeline

1. **Traffic sources** — content, ads, warm outbound, cold outbound (covered in
   a separate "8 ways to get leads" video).
2. Traffic splits by buyer readiness:
   - **Not ready / browsing** → **lead magnet** (free resource for name/email/
     phone).
   - **Ready now** → straight to the **VSL** (video sales letter).
   - Both public options should exist side by side (e.g. bio link + homepage).
3. **Lead magnet downloaders** → **30-day welcome email sequence**, every email
   pointing back to the VSL. "All roads lead to the VSL."
4. **VSL** (5–10 min) — who you help, what you do, the result, social proof,
   CTA to book.
5. **Application/survey** (Typeform, or Firm Pilot's built-in surveys feature)
   — 3–5 qualifying questions (e.g. revenue) to filter out bad-fit leads before
   they can book a call. ~80%+ of qualified applicants go on to book.
6. **Post-booking, pre-call window** (typically 3–4 days) — replaces a bare
   "see you Tuesday at 2pm" confirmation with two assets:
   - **Homework page** — long-form: offer details, process, FAQs.
   - **Proof stack email sequence** — 1–4 emails/day of case studies, results,
     proof points, each linking back to the homework page. Builds authority
     before the call.
7. **Discovery call** — three outcomes: **Closed Won**, **Closed Lost**, or
   **Follow-up** (loops back into the process).
8. **Reactivation sequence** — 3–4 months after a Closed Lost, a gentle
   check-in sequence that re-routes them back into booking. Framed explicitly
   as a real revenue source, not a write-off — many people who say no do so for
   bad reasons (price, DIY, a cheaper competitor) and come back once that
   doesn't work out.

## Key mental model

Every stage exists to **qualify, educate, and build authority** before the
human sales conversation — not to rush a stranger straight into a pitch. The
subsequent modules in this track build out each individual component (lead
magnet, welcome sequence, VSL, application, homework page, proof stack,
reactivation sequence) with its own SOP.
