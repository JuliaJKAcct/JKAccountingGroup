05Foundations

Real people, warm light, a teal grade.

Photography is the real Julia, the real team, and real client moments. No stock, no AI people, no handshakes or skylines. A consistent 4:5 crop, soft window light from one side, and a teal grade that unifies everything on dark sections.

Warm grade · window light
Teal duotone · on dark
Founder portrait · 4:5

Real team photography, shot to the recipe: temp +6 warm, saturation −8%, contrast +6, blacks lifted, highlights soft, no grain, no vignette. On dark sections, a teal duotone overlay unifies everything with the palette.

Do

  • Real Julia, team, and clients only.
  • Consistent 4:5 crop, eye-line two-thirds up.
  • Soft window light from one side.
  • Teal duotone overlay on dark sections.

Don't

  • Stock photos or AI-generated people.
  • Handshakes, skylines, or flags.
  • Cool, clinical blue grading.
  • Heavy grain or vignette.
